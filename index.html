Boyuan Deng <邓博远>


About

Boyuan Deng (邓博远) is a Master student at the BeiJing Institute of Technology. led by Prof. Sun Jian at the Beijing Institute of Technology

Boyuan's research interests lie at the intersection of perception, planning, and control. He is particularly interested in developing learning-based methods for autonomous navigation of robots in complex environments.


Research Highlights

Boyuan's research interests lie at the intersection of Reinforcement Learning (RL) and Optimal Control (OC) with a focus on real-world applications.
